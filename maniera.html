About Us

Maniera, is a large family that provides a platform for all art connoisseurs to showcase and nurture in them a budding artist. The Society holds workshops and art visits that not only help our members improve their skills, but also allow them to win a variety of competitions and bring laurels to college. The annual fest ATRANG is the most awaited event of the year as the members highlight their hard work through the exhibition.
