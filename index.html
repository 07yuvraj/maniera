About Atrang'21

Our annual festival brings together the young minds of various universities and institutions, providing them with a platform to express their talents in the best possible way. The electrifying and exciting competitions held in Atrang cater to participants from all over the country having forte in different art forms. They are awarded with exciting cash prizes and delightful goodies.
Atrang is also famous for its annual exhibition where you get to witness every form of art that you can possibly think of through the masterpieces of our talented members.
